How it works

Four simple steps — from session request to payout.

1

A shooter lists a session

A verified player posts a session request — which fish table platform they use, how long they plan to shoot, the load-up amount they need, and the profit split they're offering to backers.

2

A backer funds the session

An investor reviews the shooter's verified stats and session history, agrees to the terms, and funds the load-up. The money goes into escrow — neither side touches it until the session closes.

3

The shooter plays on their platform

FishKillerz doesn't host the games. The shooter plays on whatever fish table site they already use — Ocean King, Golden Dragon, Fish Game Kings, wherever. They submit verified before-and-after screenshots when the session ends.

4

Winnings split automatically

When the session closes, the platform calculates the split. The backer gets their load-up back plus their agreed share of profit. The shooter keeps their share. Losses carry forward as a running tab — no out-of-pocket payment required.

Common questions

Everything new users ask before their first session.

Do I have to play on FishKillerz's platform?

No. You play on whatever fish table site you already use — Ocean King, Golden Dragon, Fish Game Kings, or anything else. FishKillerz is the agreement and payout layer between you and your backer. The games themselves happen on your site.

What is a "running tab"?

If your backer funds $200 and you finish the session down $60, that $60 carries forward as a tab. Your next session's profits clear the tab first before you start earning your split again. It keeps things fair without requiring shooters to pay out of pocket after a loss.

How do I prove what I won or lost?

Before and after screenshots of your balance on the fish table platform. The app walks you through exactly what to capture. Disputed sessions go through a review process with both parties involved.

Is my money safe while a session is running?

Yes. Backer funds go into escrow when a session starts and are only released once both sides confirm the session result is verified. You're never at risk during a live session.

Can I back more than one shooter at once?

Yes. Backers can fund multiple active sessions simultaneously, spreading their investment across different shooters and different fish table platforms.

What if a shooter disappears mid-session?

Sessions have a time window agreed upfront. If a session goes unresolved past that window, the escrow is reviewed and the backer's funds are protected. Shooters who don't close out their sessions properly are flagged and removed from the platform.

How do I know if a shooter is any good before I back them?

Every shooter's profile shows their last 30 sessions — wins, losses, platforms used, average session length, and running tab balance. You have full visibility before committing a single dollar.
